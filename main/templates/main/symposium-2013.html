Fruitful Minds Symposium on Nutrition Education

1 – 4 PM Sunday, April 21, 2013
102 Wurster Hall [map]

Fruitful Minds is a non-profit student group on campus that provides access to nutrition education for children throughout the Bay Area. Our main focus is to inspire young students to be the best they can be through healthy choices while also providing college students with professional development.


Speakers

  • Kyle Cornforth — Director of The Edible Schoolyard (who will also be presenting results from a Center for Weight and Health study on the Fruitful Minds program)
  • Luis Rodriguez — Dietitian at the UCSF Benioff Children's Hospital and Nutritionist at the WATCH (Weight Assessment for Teen and Child Health) Clinic
  • Joanne Ikeda — Vice President of the Society on Nutrition Education and Behavior (SNEB), former UC Berkeley professor, and nutrition consultant to the Cartoon Network
  • Linda Shak — Representative from the Prevention Institute
  • Sophie Johnson — Executive Director of Choice Lunch, a healthy lunch program used in over 250 schools in Northern and Southern California

At the end of the event, there will be a student and community group fair to let participants explore opportunities in nutrition education and health advocacy and promotion at UC Berkeley.
